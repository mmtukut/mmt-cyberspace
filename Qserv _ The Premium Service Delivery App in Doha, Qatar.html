How Did Qserv Begin

In 2015, the idea to create a unique service requesting platform dedicated to the people of Qatar was firstborn. It all started as a pen and paper idea which eventually blossomed to the conceptualization of Qserv. From originally being a Qatari-focused project, the idea has now grown into a service app for the people. It’s all about bringing the best to those who are here in Qatar.

The Qserv team is a group of the brightest minds working together from designers to investors, developers to service providers. Qserv believes that great things come from the smallest of ideas and now it is here. The best service delivery app for you, bringing everything you need with just a tap.
